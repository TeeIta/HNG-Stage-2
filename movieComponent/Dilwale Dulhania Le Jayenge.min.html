"Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge," often abbreviated as DDLJ, is a timeless Bollywood classic and romantic drama film released in 1995. Directed by Aditya Chopra, the movie tells the story of Raj and Simran, played by Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol, as they meet and fall in love during a European vacation. However, their love faces numerous challenges, particularly the strong opposition of Simran's father.
